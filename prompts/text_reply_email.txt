## 返信メールの基本構成

### 冒頭パターン
**受信確認:**
- "Thank you for your email."
- "Thank you for reaching out."
- "Thank you for getting back to me."

**前回の内容への言及:**
- "Regarding your question about..."
- "In response to your inquiry..."
- "Following up on our conversation..."

### 本文の表現パターン
**情報提供:**
- "Please find attached..."
- "As requested, here is..."
- "I'd like to inform you that..."

**依頼:**
- "Could you please...?"
- "Would it be possible to...?"
- "I would appreciate it if you could..."

**確認:**
- "Just to confirm..."
- "To clarify..."
- "Could you please confirm...?"

**承諾:**
- "I'd be happy to..."
- "That works for me."
- "I can certainly do that."

**断り:**
- "Unfortunately, I'm unable to..."
- "I'm afraid that won't be possible because..."
- "I appreciate the offer, but..."

### 結びパターン
**フォーマル:**
- "Best regards,"
- "Sincerely,"
- "Kind regards,"

**ビジネスカジュアル:**
- "Best,"
- "Thanks,"
- "Cheers," (UK寄り)

**次のアクション示唆:**
- "Please let me know if you have any questions."
- "I look forward to hearing from you."
- "Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything else."

## トーン別のガイドライン

### フォーマル（上司、顧客、初対面）
- "Dear Mr./Ms. [姓]," で始める
- would/could を多用
- "I would appreciate..." "Could you kindly..."
- "Best regards," で締める

### ニュートラル（同僚、取引先）
- "Hi [名]," または "Hello [名],"
- 適度な丁寧さ
- "Please let me know..." "I'll..."
- "Best," または "Thanks," で締める

### カジュアル（親しい同僚）
- "Hi [名]," または "Hey [名],"
- 短い文、縮約形OK
- "Thanks!" "Sure!"
- "Cheers," または "Talk soon,"
