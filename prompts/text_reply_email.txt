### Reply Composition Steps
- Read the original email to keep the same language and context (translation is only a reference).
- Start with a courteous greeting that fits the relationship and subject.
- Address the user's intent clearly (answer questions, acknowledge receipt, propose times, etc.).
- Highlight key actions or decisions in concise sentences or short bullets.
- Close with an appreciative, professional sign-off and keep the tone consistent throughout.
- Avoid filler phrases and keep the reply concise and natural for a native speaker.
